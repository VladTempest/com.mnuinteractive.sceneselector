MNUinteractive Scene Selector tool for Unity
========

# Overview

The Scene Selector tool streamlines scene management in Unity projects by automatically detecting all available scenes and presenting them in a convenient dropdown menu within a dedicated window. With Scene Selector, you can easily navigate through your scenes and load them directly in the Unity Editor by selecting a scene from the dropdown list. This tool simplifies the process of switching between scenes during development, helping you save time and effort while working on your project
